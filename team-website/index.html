What is iGEM?

The International Genetic Engineering Machine competition (iGEM) is a global synthetic biology competition where students tackle real-world problems with engineered biology. But it’s more than just research: it’s about entrepreneurship, communication, and creating real impact.

iGEM fosters collaboration, creativity, and innovation, empowering participants to make a positive impact on the world.

What do we do?

We’re a team of passionate students using synthetic biology to create solutions that improve lives. With backgrounds in science and engineering, we combine research, education, and community outreach to make a lasting impact.
